## 2. Key Themes to Highlight

### A. Trust (No Hallucinations)
*   *Narrative*: "This isn't ChatGPT guessing your revenue. This is a secure system running exact math on your actual database."
*   *Proof Point*: Show the "Data Used" citation in the answer.

### B. Speed (Conversational Analytics)
*   *Narrative*: "It's like having your best analyst sitting next to you, 24/7."
*   *Proof Point*: Ask a rapid-fire sequence: "Revenue?" -> "By Product?" -> "Top 5 only?"

### C. Safety (Budget & Control)
*   *Narrative*: "You have full control. You can see exactly how much it costs and stop it instantly."
*   *Proof Point*: Point to the "Budget Bar" in the sidebar.

## 3. The Demo Flow (Script)

### Scenario: The Monday Morning Review
*   **User**: "Okay, let's see how we closed the quarter. **Show me total revenue vs forecast.**"
    *   *Result*: A Bar Chart appears.
    *   *Commentary*: "Instant visibility. No Excel vlookups."

*   **User**: "Looks like we missed. **Where is the gap coming from? Drill down by Region.**"
    *   *Result*: A breakdown chart.
    *   *Commentary*: "Immediate root cause analysis. It understood 'Drill down'."

*   **User**: "Okay, focus on the East region. **Show me the trend.**"
    *   *Result*: A Line Chart for East Region.
    *   *Commentary*: "It keeps the context. We just navigated from High-level -> Root Cause -> Trend in 30 seconds."
